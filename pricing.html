Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a manicure and pedicure?

For optimal nail health and maintenance, we recommend getting a manicure every 2 to 3 weeks and a pedicure every 4 to 6 weeks.

Are your nail products and tools safe?

Yes, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our clients. All our nail products and tools are sourced from reputable brands and manufacturers, adhering to strict quality and safety standards.

How do I take care of my nails between appointments?

To maintain your nails between appointments, we recommend keeping them clean, moisturized, and avoiding harsh chemicals. Regularly apply cuticle oil and protect your nails from excessive moisture.
