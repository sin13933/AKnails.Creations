Biogel Nails

Biogel nails refer to a type of artificial nail enhancement that is made using a gel-like substance. These nails are designed to provide a durable and long-lasting solution for individuals who want to enhance the appearance of their natural nails. They offer a wide range of colors and finishes, allowing individuals to customize their nails according to their preferences.

Gel Overlay

A gel overlay is a popular technique used to strengthen and protect natural nails. It involves applying a thin layer of gel onto the natural nails, which is then cured under a UV or LED lamp. The gel overlay provides added strength, durability, and shine to the nails, making them less prone to breaking or splitting. This technique is often chosen by individuals who prefer a natural-looking nail enhancement that is easy to maintain.
